### Legal Compliance
- **CFAA (Computer Fraud and Abuse Act)**: Do NOT use on systems without authorization
- **ECPA (Electronic Communications Privacy Act)**: Do NOT intercept communications without consent
- **ToS Violation**: Platform usage violations carry legal liability
- **Local Laws**: Verify compliance with your jurisdiction's cybersecurity laws

### Before Using
1. Obtain explicit written authorization from account/system owner
2. Verify you have legal right to perform operations
3. Document authorization date, scope, and authorized user accounts
4. Keep audit logs of all activity
5. Report results only to authorized personnel

### Proxy & Anonymity
- Use only for legitimate OPSEC (not to evade law enforcement)
- Tor/VPN should comply with local laws
- Never use proxies to violate account terms of service
- Rate limiting prevents detection while respecting service limits

### Incident Disclosure
- Discovered vulnerabilities: Report to platform security team first
- Give 90 days for patch before public disclosure
- Do NOT publicly disclose account credentials
- Document and preserve evidence legally
